Garden

Definition

A garden is a planned space, usually outdoors, set aside for the display,cultivation, or enjoyment of plants and other forms of nature.The garden can incorporate both natural and man-made materials.The most common form today is a residential garden, but the term garden hastraditionally been a more general one. Zoos, which display wild animals insimulated natural habitats, were formerly called zoological gardens.Western gardens are almost universally based on plants, with garden oftensignifying a shortened form of botanical garden. Some traditional types ofeastern gardens, such as Zen gardens, use plants sparsely or not at all.
